01 Live ✦

Forseti

Control live actions.

Input Agent intent A typed request arrives with target, operation, and risk context.
Decision Policy engine Rules decide allow, deny, or whether human approval is required.
Escalation Approval routing High-risk work pauses until the right approvers sign off.
Release Approved action Execution proceeds with a durable audit trail and trace output.
Traces → seeded scenarios
02 Live ◈

Tyche

Replay and compare.

Intake Scenario spec A trace, incident, or prompt set becomes a replayable scenario bundle.
Execution Seeded runners Strategies compete under fixed conditions so results stay comparable.
Evidence Replay bundle Outputs capture decisions, tool calls, state changes, and score signals.
Output Policy packs Winning playbooks become reusable policies and delivery-ready patterns.
Validated patterns → delivery room
03 Coming Soon ∩

Valhalla

Structured delivery.

Intake Spec intake The room clarifies scope, risks, and completion criteria before work starts.
Execution Role loop Named specialist roles plan, build, review, and refine against the brief.
Control Human review Operators inspect convergence, evidence, and risk before release.
Outcome Delivered change Validated work ships through a controlled room instead of an open-ended agent loop.
Tyche feeds replay-backed policy packs back into Forseti while also preparing Valhalla for disciplined delivery.
